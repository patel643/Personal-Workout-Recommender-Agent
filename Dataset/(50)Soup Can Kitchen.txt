Soup Can Kitchen

If you don’t have any weights to use, grab a couple of soup cans or anything similar.  You can substitute a playground ball for a medicine ball)  Feel free to improvise!

2 Rounds — 15 Reps Each (unless otherwise stated)


15 to each side:  Medicine ball, lay on SIDE, ball overhead.  Now turn and sit up to the center and reach the medicine ball between ankles.  Then lay all the way back on your other side. Repeat.

15 each side Weighted Half Wiper (medium weight in one hand and medicine ball between shins — Lay on back. Bring weight to one side and straight legs to the other.)

15 each side Reverse Lunge with Side Bend (Hold two medium weights overhead.  Step back, knee to floor then lean/bend sideways — same side as your back leg — straighten up and stand up)

15 each leg:  Plank Leg Swings, Plank hands wide, bring right leg straight out to side near right hand. Now swing it underneath you and up to outside of left hand.  Do 15 then switch sides.

15 total:  Mixed Grip Inverted Rows (one hand over one under — second round switch hand positions)

15 each arm — Feet Elevated Alternating Dumbbell Chest Press (lay on bench, knees bent, feet in air)

15 back and forth:  Medicine Ball (or Stability Ball — easier — Transfer (transfer ball between hands and ankles, lower both toward floor, repeat)

25 each side - Seated Boxer Twist (hold weight)

25 each side - Half Kneeling Wood Choppers

Do this 5 times:  Lay on back, arms at side.  Lift legs 6 inches off floor and CIRCLE 5 times to the right, 5 times to the left;  Then do 10 straight leg lifts in the air.

Lay on side knees curled to chest, straighten top leg out 90 degrees.  40 lifts, 10 circles fwd, 10 circles backward.  Repeat on other side
