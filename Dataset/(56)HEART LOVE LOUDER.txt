HEART LOVE LOUDER

Warm Up
50 High Knees, 50 Jacks
25 Squats
25 Jump Squats
20 Right Front Lunge
20 Right Side Lunge
20 Left Front Lunge
20 Left Side Lunge
40 Jump & Switch Lunges
20 X Three Squats, 180 Degree Jump turn
30 X Two Squats, 180 Degree Jump turn
40 X One Squat, 180 Degree Jump turn

Ladder:

100 Each Leg Front Kick (or Jump Rope 100 skips each leg)
Repeat above except only 50 each leg & add: 5 Tuck Jump Sprawls
Repeat all of the above & add: 100 Each Leg Round Kick (or 100 skips each leg)
Repeat all above except only 50 each leg & add: 10 times do 5 X-Jumps; Roll Back Stand up
Repeat all of the above & add: 20 Weighted Squats
Repeat all of the above & add:  20 Each Leg Single Leg Romanian Dead Lifts
Repeat all of the above & add:  50 Moguls (plank position, jump twist legs side to side)

Fin.
